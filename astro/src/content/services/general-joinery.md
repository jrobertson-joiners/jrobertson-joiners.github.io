## General Joinery Services in Stirling

From fitting a single door to crafting bespoke staircases, our core joinery services cover everything you need to enhance, repair, or transform your home's interior and exterior woodwork.

### What We Do

#### Doors & Windows
- Internal door supply and fitting
- External door replacement and security upgrades
- Door frame repairs and adjustments
- Sash window restoration
- Box frame repairs and draught-proofing

#### Flooring
- Hardwood floor installation
- Engineered board fitting
- Floor sanding and refinishing
- Subfloor repairs and preparation
- Transition strips and thresholds

#### Staircases
- Bespoke staircase design and manufacture
- Staircase renovations and cladding
- Balustrade and handrail installation
- Under-stair storage solutions
- Loft ladder and hatch installation

#### Internal Finishings
- Skirting boards and architraves
- Window boards and reveals
- Picture rails and dado rails
- Built-in wardrobes and storage
- Shelving and alcove units

#### External Joinery
- Decking installation and repairs
- Garden sheds and outbuildings
- Fencing and gates
- Pergolas and outdoor structures
- Fascias and soffits

### Our Specialities

Beyond everyday joinery, we have particular experience in:

- **Heritage restoration** – Sympathetic repairs to period features in older properties
- **Traditional Victorian shutters** – Restoration and new installations
- **Custom furniture** – One-off pieces designed to fit your space exactly
- **Structural timber work** – Roof timbers, floor joists, and load-bearing frameworks

### Service Areas

Based in Stirling (FK8), we work throughout Central Scotland including Bridge of Allan, Dunblane, Bannockburn, Doune, Callander, Aberfoyle, and the Trossachs area.
