## Project Overview

*This is a placeholder project. Real project details coming soon.*

A comprehensive kitchen renovation in a period townhouse in central Stirling. The homeowners wanted a modern, functional kitchen that would complement the character of their Victorian property.

### Scope of Work

- Full strip-out of existing kitchen
- New electrical layout and lighting design
- Handleless cabinet installation
- Quartz worktop templating and fitting
- Integrated appliances including built-in oven, induction hob, and dishwasher
- Flooring installation
- Decoration and finishing touches

### The Result

The finished kitchen provides a bright, modern cooking space with plenty of storage and worktop area, while the careful colour palette respects the home's traditional character.
