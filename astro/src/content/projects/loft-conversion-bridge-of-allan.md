## Project Overview

*This is a placeholder project. Real project details coming soon.*

This loft conversion transformed unused attic space into a stunning master bedroom suite complete with en-suite shower room and built-in storage.

### Scope of Work

- Structural survey and Building Warrant application
- Rear dormer construction
- New staircase design and installation
- Floor strengthening
- Velux windows to front elevation
- En-suite plumbing and tiling
- Built-in wardrobes and storage
- Electrical installation including spotlights and sockets
- Full decoration to client specification

### The Result

The homeowners gained a private retreat on the top floor, with excellent natural light from the new dormer and Velux windows. The carefully planned layout maximises the available space while maintaining comfortable headroom throughout.
