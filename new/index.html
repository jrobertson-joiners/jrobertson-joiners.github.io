About the team

Joiners in Stirling since 2016, proud of every finish

We pride ourselves on customer satisfaction from idea to completion. As a joinery-led building contractor, we tailor every job to suit your individual needs, combining hands-on carpentry with full project management.

Jamie Robertson Joiners has been trading since March 2016, growing from a small workshop serving neighbours into a trusted team covering Stirling and the wider Central Scotland area. With more than a decade of hands-on experience across house building, renovations, and bespoke furniture, we bring the same care to every project, whatever the size.

Our roots are in joinery - from structural timber work and bespoke kitchens to internal finishings and custom storage - but we also coordinate the other trades needed to take your project from first ideas through to completion. Working with a trusted network of electricians, plumbers, plasterers, and decorators, we can deliver full contract construction builds and refurbishments, so you have one point of contact from start to finish.

Every job is carried out safely, in line with Building Regulations, and with a strong focus on value, quality, and tidy workmanship. Whether it's modernising a townhouse or building out a larger extension, you can count on practical advice, clear timelines, and work that stands up to everyday life.
